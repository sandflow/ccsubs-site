At its telecon on 2025-03-31 at 15:00 UTC, CCSUBS will host a discussion on the [European Accessibility Act (EAA)](https://commission.europa.eu/strategy-and-policy/policies/justice-and-fundamental-rights/disability/union-equality-strategy-rights-persons-disabilities-2021-2030/european-accessibility-act_en) and what it means for subtitles and captions.

[Join the group today](/join) to take part in the discussion!

Background: by June 2025 the accessibility requirements of the EAA must be implemented in each EU member state. The discussion is intended to provide a brief overview of what this means for subtitles and captions.
